Supra Saiyans Lore

Legends of the Digital Warriors

The Ancient Prophecy

In the vast expanse of the digital cosmos, a prophecy spoke of warriors who would transcend the boundaries between reality and blockchain. These legendary beings, known as Supra Saiyans, possess the power to harness the infinite energy of decentralized networks.

The Dreaming Dragons

Guardian spirits of the SupraVerse, the Dreaming Dragons existed in the space between sleep and consciousness, weaving the fabric of digital dreams into reality. Perfection is a dream, perfection is a direction, perfection is a Paradox

The Great Awakening

When the Supra Network emerged, it created the perfect conditions for the Supra Saiyans to manifest in the SupraVerse. Through adventures and adversities, ordinary traders and holders become heroes and experts at channeling the legendary energy to join the ranks of the digital warriors.

The Expansion

United by the code of honor, ethical purity, transparency and the pursuit of financial freedom, the Supra Saiyans work together to build a decentralized ecosystem where everyone can achieve their highest potential. The journey continues as more warriors awaken to their true power. Join Us!
